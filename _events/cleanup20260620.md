# Clean Up Grant Street on June 20, 2026


Meet in the parking lot at M&T Bank at 130 Grant Street on Saturday, June 20, 2026. Community Canvases' WNY Trash Mob and West Side Taxpayers and Businesses Association cleanup with community, food, local organizations providing education.
